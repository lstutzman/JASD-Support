Privacy Policy

How we protect your data and privacy

Last updated: December 2024

Overview

JASD Property Analyzer is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our property investment analysis application.

Information We Collect

Our app is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information necessary to provide our services:

  • Property Data: Address information, purchase prices, rental income, and investment calculations you enter into the app
  • Usage Analytics: Basic app usage statistics to improve functionality (anonymized)
  • Support Communications: Information you provide when contacting our support team
  • Crash Reports: Technical data to help us fix bugs and improve app stability

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Provide property investment calculations and analysis
  • Store your property projects locally on your device
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Provide customer support when requested
  • Ensure app security and prevent misuse

Data Storage and Security

We prioritize your data security:

  • Local Storage: All your property data is stored locally on your device using SQLite database
  • No Cloud Sync: Your property information is never uploaded to our servers
  • Encryption: Data is encrypted using iOS security frameworks
  • Device Control: You have complete control over your data and can delete it at any time

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services with their own privacy policies:

  • getAddress API: For UK postcode lookup (only postcode data is shared)
  • Bank of England API: For current mortgage base rates (no personal data shared)
  • Apple App Store: For app distribution and analytics

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored in the app
  • Delete your data by uninstalling the app
  • Contact us about privacy concerns
  • Opt out of analytics (through iOS settings)

Data Retention

Your property data is retained locally on your device until you choose to delete it or uninstall the app. Support communications are retained for up to 2 years to provide ongoing assistance.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the app or our website. Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

International Users

While designed for the UK property market, our app may be available in other countries. Data protection laws may vary by jurisdiction, but we apply consistent privacy standards globally.

Contact Us

Note: This app is designed for informational purposes only and does not constitute financial advice. Always consult qualified professionals for investment decisions.
