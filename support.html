Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the free tier?

The free tier allows you to analyze up to 3 property projects using Buy-to-Let calculations only. You'll need to enter addresses manually and use standard mortgage rates. Perfect for getting started with property investment analysis.

What do I get with JASD Standard (£49.99/year)?

Standard subscription includes unlimited property projects, UK postcode lookup with auto-complete, real-time Bank of England mortgage rates, all investment strategy types (HMO, Serviced Accommodation, Buy-to-Flip, Rent-to-Rent), and premium support.

How do I get started with property analysis?

Simply tap the "+" button on the main screen to create a new property project. Enter the property address (with postcode lookup available in Standard tier), then fill in your purchase price and investment details. The app will automatically calculate your returns.

How accurate are the mortgage rate calculations?

Our mortgage rates are based on real-time Bank of England base rates with industry-standard LTV (Loan-to-Value) risk premiums. Rates update automatically and reflect current market conditions for different property types.

What investment strategies are available?

Free tier: Buy-to-Let only. Standard tier: All strategies including HMO (House in Multiple Occupation), Serviced Accommodation (Airbnb-style), Buy-to-Flip, and Rent-to-Rent. Each calculation is tailored to the specific investment type with appropriate risk calculations.

Is my data saved automatically?

Yes, the app features intelligent auto-save that protects your work as you type. All data is stored securely on your device and works offline.

Does the app work offline?

Yes, once you've created property projects, all calculations and data work completely offline. You only need internet for UK address lookup (Standard tier) and updating mortgage rates (Standard tier).

How do I upgrade to Standard subscription?

Open the app settings menu (hamburger icon) and tap "JASD Standard Subscription". You can manage your subscription through the App Store at any time.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your Standard subscription anytime through your App Store account settings. You'll continue to have access to Standard features until the end of your billing period.

How do I delete a property project?

Open the property project and look for the delete button (−) in the toolbar. This button only appears for saved projects and includes a confirmation dialog for safety.
